STSAFE-A120_Symmetric_key_establishment_AES-256_CCM

This example illustrates a symmetric key (AES-256 CCM) establishment and usage in STSAFE-A1xx Secure Element. It uses the STSAFE Middleware APIs to a establish a key and encrypt then decrypt a message.
